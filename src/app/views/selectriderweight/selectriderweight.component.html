Why does weight matter?

Step 1 / 7


Rider weight and board feel.

Your board cannot feel your height. It can only feel your weight. Each board has been designed to suit a certain rider weight. Ideally your weight should be in just the middle between the minimum and the maximum recommended rider weight for the board. That is what the board has been designed for.


If you prefer a softer feel you should chose a board where your weight is closer to the maximum recommended rider weight. And if you prefer a stiffer feel you should chose a board where your weight is closer to the minimum recommended rider weight.


Riding a too soft board can make the board loose grip and feel wobbly. Riding a too stiff board will be hard to press and manouver.
